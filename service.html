A note for parents

Although our primary recipient of our service is the student, we understand the fact that parents have as many dreams and needs to be fulfilled, if not more. The shift to studying abroad is for certain a happy occasion to the entire family, signifying the culmination of years of upbringing for a bright future. However, this shift is fraught with changes affecting students and parents alike. We realize that such a change can be confusing and stressful. Hence, this is why we bid parents welcome during the initial strategy session to gain some clarity of mind to whatever inquiry they may.
