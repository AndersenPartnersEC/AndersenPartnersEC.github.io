A Team With Diverse Backgrounds

The founders - Alessandro, David and Hanlin graduated from top universities in Europe and Asia and have extensive experience in the field of education, consulting and finance.

Our Team

Meet Our Top Consultants

Alessandro Buffoli

CEO-Founder

  After a degree in Business administration and the prestigious CEMS Master in International Management that he attained studying at HKUST, Alessandro is currently working towards a PhD in Consumer Behavior - Marketing at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. In applying for his PhD he was awarded the highly coveted HKPFS scholarship. He speaks 4 languages and is currently in the process learning an intermediate level of Mandarin Chinese. During his life he lived in different countries in Europe and Asia, including but not limited to Italy, China, France and Austria.

  Alessandro worked as a tutor for GMAT and GRE exams, developing a strong expertise in the college admission processes worldwide and in advising his students in the most efficient ways to bring their applications up to a level beyond what they may have archived themselves. He has helped students to get admitted to INSEAD, Yale University, HEC Paris and other top institutions. Not only is Alessandro an expert in aiding students to develop their narrative, but also in defining their leadership skills and their extracurricular profiles. He strongly believes there is nothing as rewarding as watching a student gain confidence in their skills and succeeding in the college admissions process.

David Zhu Andersen

Co-Founder

  David grew up in Denmark, where he graduated at the well recognized Copenhagen Business School (CBS) that is regularly considered among the top 5 business schools worldwide by Eduniversal ranking. Like Hanlin and Alessandro, he also attained the CEMS Master in International Management, exchanging for one semester at HKUST. David is half Chinese and has substantial international exposure, for instance, in light of regular trips to China and a global network. He has an interest in project management, education, finance, and management, and he is currently working for Denmark’s financial service company.


  Given his professional expertise and that his family has always been involved in the field of higher education, with strong ties to Duke University, MIT, and Tsinghua University among others, he has a perfect knowledge of a vast number of university programs in Europe. His expertise lies in matching the students with the colleges that are the most suitable to their profiles. He speaks 4 languages and in light of his background has lived in many different countries including China, Denmark, England, and the US.

Hanlin Chen

Co-Founder

  Hanlin graduated from The University of Sydney where he studied the cosmopolitan “CEMS Master in Management” program. He was born in China, then moving to Germany with his family when he was young. Currently he is working as a consultant for the “Fortune 500” company Accenture. During his study and work career he developed a strong experience in the education field. He speaks 3 languages and has lived, studied, and worked in a large number of countries including Australia, China, Austria, Germany.


  Thanks to his Sino-European background he is extremely skilled in understanding what is needed for Chinese students to be successfully admitted to Western universities. Given his profession as a consultant, he can draft remarkably precise work plans to solve even the most complicated situations, increasing exponentially the chances of his students to study in the universities of their dreams. His strategy, decision-making skills and his passion for education and consulting explain his commitment to offering quality advisory services.
