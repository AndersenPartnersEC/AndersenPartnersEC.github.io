The Premium Package

                   

If you are ready to discover your profile-matching universities, need an overview of the application process, and require a final grammar check then this is the bundle for you!

What's Included

1.

Client Questionnaire

We send out targeted questions. Take your time so that we get a complete picture of you. From this we work on matching you to your perfect university opportunities.

2.

Discovery / Strategy Session

In addition to the questionnaire we would like to get an in-depth understanding of your profile through face to face interaction.

3.

Identify areas of improvement / Match universities

After a thorough investigation of your profile, we advise on areas of improvement and scrutinize the global environment for your perfect university.

Application action plan

At this point we design the strategy of the primary tasks that goes into the application process and support in the identification of the required documents.

Careful grammar check

To complete the process we review the grammar of the required content documents you have been working on writing for the university.
