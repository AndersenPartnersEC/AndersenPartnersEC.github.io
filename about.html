Extensive Experience

Anderson & Partners Admittance Consulting is an organization whose mission is to help students to get admitted to prestigious universities worldwide.

Client Matchmakers

We offer an array of services for students who want to apply for bachelors or masters abroad. We are prepared to support customers of different age groups and with different needs, selecting the best possible path to accomplish our students’ requests. Given our European background, we are deeply specialized in aiding our clients to successfully be admitted to university in Europe, but we can help also with the applications for American and Asian universities.

Value proposition

Anderson & Partners Admittance Consulting is the perfect choice if you are a student who wants to remarkably improve your chances to be admitted to a top graduate or postgraduate program abroad. We are aware of the complicated admission procedures that the universities use to select their students, and we are glad to use all our expertise and skills to help you to achieve your desired result.

Core values

In our company our clients always come first. We are happy to assess each individual situation in depth and to help our students to select the universities right for them. Persistence, honesty, integrity and patience are just some of the qualities that we look for in our collaborators and that we desire to manifest to our customers.

Head Office

Our head office is located in Hong Kong, China. Given that many of our customers come from other, for example, Chinese cities we offer the opportunity to schedule video meetings with us through platforms such as WeChat or DingDing.
