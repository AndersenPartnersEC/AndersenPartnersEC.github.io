Welcome to Andersen & Partners

Extensive Experience

  Anderson & Partners Educational Consulting is an educational development company that aims at helping Chinese students to get admitted to the most prestigious colleges worldwide.

  The founders - Alessandro, David and Hanlin – graduated from top universities in Europe and Asia and have extensive experience in the field of education, consulting, finance and banking. Through fully individualized advising we aspire to support our customers' applications to their chosen universities and to boost their chances to be admitted. Our mission is to assist our students to choose and to enter the schools of their dreams!

Matchmakers

We offer a vast array of services for students who want to apply for bachelors or masters abroad. We are prepared to support customers of different age groups and with different needs, selecting the best possible path to accomplish our students’ requests. Given the European background of the firm, we are deeply specialized in aiding our clients to successfully be admitted to colleges in Europe, but we can help also with the applications for American and Asian universities.
